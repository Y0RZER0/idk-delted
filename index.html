- Creating new world

You may start playing by creating "World" where you will play on, Click "single player" then "New world" and select "Gamemode", "Difficulty" and "World option"

Gamemode is the mode you will play on like "Survival mode, you will need to find resource and foods." or "Creative mode, Fly freely around the map and build anything you want. Got infinite resource!!!"

Difficulty represent how hard of the game, you should choose easy (recommend for first time playing) --ABout Difficulty, in Hard mode you can starve to death, Mob (Word we used to mention monster without mentioning specific type) will increase damage. And in peaceful mode, You don't need food and Mob won't attack you!!

First time surviving

Okay, Create a world and set the Difficulty to "easy" (or any others, But easy is recommend) --BTW remember to set your gamemode as survival!


Control

How to move, Attack, Mine and place block. Use the key W, A, S, D (on your keyboard obviously) To move (W - forward, S - backward, A - left, D - right). And to rotate your head around, Move your mouse (Like those First-Person-Shooter game)

How to mine, Place block. To attack kindly left click your mouse once then release (DONE, you just hitted 1 time) if you are trying to attack something (like cow, Kill it for meat) Go near them, Attack range is 3 blocks far, If you are further than that, You can't hit them. To mine is same with attack, But you will need to hold left click (Aim at block, Press and hold left click.) To place block, Make sure that block is in your main hand (see picture) then right click.

Others control- Press E to open inventory, Use mouse wheel to scroll (or numbers key 1-9) to select item in hotbar (see picture)


- Prepare for the night

Mob (monster that attack you) Will spawn at night, In the cave and any dark area (So you are safe in day time) So you will want to get wood (Go near it and Mine/Punch call it whatever u want) Then the wood will drop after you punch it for a while, Go near it then it will come in your inventory!

Minecraft, I'm sure almost everyone have heard of this game.

Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can build creatively, Play with friend, Role playing, Gather resource to survive and a lot more!

Minecraft got 3 main modes that is "Survival", "Creative" and "Hardcore".

In Survival mode you have to find food, gather resource and fence off monsters to survive.

In Creative mode you can fy around and build anything you want, maybe a house or a little farm or even THE WHOLE CASTLE.

In Hardcore mode, This mode is same with survival but you only got 1 life, If you die then you will turn into Spectator mode. In that mode you can fly around but can't do anything with the world (being like ghost, Can only Spectate but can't interact anymore)
